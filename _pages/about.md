I'm currently working as **Data Services Librarian** in [**SMU Libraries**](https://library.smu.edu.sg/), where I support computational methods of data analysis in academic research to meet the teaching, research, and learning needs of the SMU community, which includes faculty, students, and researchers. 

When not reading or tinkering on stuff in Python or R, I occasionally do some volunteer teaching for the folks at [The Carpentries](https://carpentries.org/index.html).
