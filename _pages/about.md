I am currently a <ins>Senior Librarian</ins> with the <ins>Research and Data Services</ins> team in [**SMU Libraries**](https://library.smu.edu.sg/), where I support computational methods of data analysis in academic research to meet the teaching, research, and learning needs of the SMU community, which includes faculty, students, and researchers. I graduated from Nanyang Technological University in 2018 with a MSc in Information Studies.

When not reading or tinkering on stuff in Python or R, I occasionally do some volunteer teaching for the folks at [The Carpentries](https://carpentries.org/index.html).
