*Selected list of teaching activities and workshops that I conducted*

2024
------
- 30 May 2024 - '[Try it Together: Transcribe Your Audio with Whisper API](https://ink.library.smu.edu.sg/ai_research_week/Programme/Programme/3/)'
- 28 May 2024 - '[Try it together - AI-powered search tools: Elicit, SciSpace, Connected Papers, Researchrabbit, and Undermind.ai](https://ink.library.smu.edu.sg/ai_research_week/Programme/Programme/2/)' - with co-instructors [Aaron Tay](http://musingsaboutlibrarianship.blogspot.com/) and Ooi Kooi Cheng
- Jan to Feb 2024 - '[Introductory R for Social Sciences](https://bellaratmelia.quarto.pub/intro-r-socsci/)' - Second run of the in-person and hands-on technical workshop jointly organized by SMU Libraries and the School of Social Sciences. The course was expanded into five weekly 120-minute sessions.


2023
------
- 29-30 Nov 2023 - '[Data Carpentry (Social Sciences with R) for SMU](https://rsangole.github.io/2023-11-15-cmu-online/)' (Open only to PhD students and research staff of SMU)
- 15-17 Nov 2023 - '[Data Carpentry (Social Sciences with R) for Carnegie Mellon University](https://rsangole.github.io/2023-11-15-cmu-online/)'
- Aug to Sep 2023 - 'Introductory R for Social Sciences' - First run of the in-person and hands-on technical workshop jointly organized by SMU Libraries and the School of Social Sciences. Comprising four weekly 90-minute sessions, the workshop aims to introduce R for social science research. It targets students interested in using R for research or enrolled in social science courses, as well as those with some R experience looking to refresh their knowledge.
- 30 May - 2 Jun 2023 - '[Software Carpentry (Python) for TU Delft/University of Twente](https://4turesearchdata-carpentries.github.io/2023-05-30-Twente/)'
- 6-9 Nov 2023 - '[Software Carpentry (Python) for TU Delft/University of Twente](https://4turesearchdata-carpentries.github.io/2023-11-06-twente-online/)'
