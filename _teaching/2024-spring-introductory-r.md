This is the second run of the in-person and hands-on technical workshop jointly organized by SMU Libraries and the School of Social Sciences. The main goal is still the same, which is to introduce R in the context of its use for social science research. Unlike the previous run, this time the workshop comprises five 120-minute long sessions. 

The target audience of this workshop was:
- Students who are interested to use R to run research or are enrolled in any of the following social science courses are encouraged to attend these workshops. 
- Students who have some experience using R may also like to attend the workshops to refresh their knowledge.

Website for this run is available in my Quarto Pub: https://bellaratmelia.quarto.pub/intro-r-socsci/
