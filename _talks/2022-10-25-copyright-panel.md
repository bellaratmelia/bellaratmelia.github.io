I moderated a panel discussion on Text and Data Mining for the [SAUL Copyright Seminar 2022](https://ink.library.smu.edu.sg/saulcopyright2022/), a collaborative effort by Singapore Management University Libraries and the Singapore Alliance of University Libraries (SAUL). The session's main theme was to explore the implications of the new amendments in Singapore Copyright Law, specifically the exception for text and data mining (TDM), on academics and librarians in Singapore.
