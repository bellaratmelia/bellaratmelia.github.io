I delivered a keynote session in [Access Lab 2024](https://www.openathens.net/access-lab/), outlining the implications of AI for academic libraries. I shared insights into how SMU Libraries has proactively addressed and navigated these implications. A key highlight of the session was on SMU Libraries' inaugural AI hackathon, '[Generate Your L(AI)brary](https://researchguides.smu.edu.sg/hackathon-2023),' held in August 2023, and the subsequent research project that followed.
